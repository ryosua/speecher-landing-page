Why Speecher?

The Problem

Does public speaking make you nervous? Do you fear saying something embarrassing, or being judged by your peers? Have you ever forgot what you wanted to say when you were presenting?

Build Confidence

Speecher will help you build the confidence you need by helping you outline speeches effectively. Prepare note cards with the app now.

Features

Organize Your Thoughts

Speecher helps you condense your script into note cards and keywords to help you improve memory and avoid losing your place in front of your peers.

Dial in your timing

Practicing with Speecher will help you dial in your timing, so that your speech or presentation is just the right length.

Record your Speeches

Speecher allows you to record and play back your speeches so you can sound more confident and prepared.
